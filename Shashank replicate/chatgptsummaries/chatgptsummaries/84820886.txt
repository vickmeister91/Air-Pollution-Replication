The court order in question pertains to a petition filed under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. The petitioner sought to set aside an order passed by the Additional Sessions Judge and confirming an order passed by the Additional District Magistrate. The crux of the matter was the compliance of an industry with the provisions of the Air and Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Acts.

The court had directed the State of Punjab to submit a report regarding the industry's compliance with the aforementioned Acts. The report filed by the Punjab Pollution Control Board stated that no action was required to be taken against the industry as it complied with the prescribed norms for the discharge of effluent/emissions. The court, after perusing the report, dismissed the petition as no further action was warranted.
