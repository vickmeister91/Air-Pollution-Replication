The High Court of Madhya Pradesh has granted bail to three individuals, Pradeep Patel, Sunil Kumar Mehra, and Rohit Rathore, who were charged with illegal possession and transportation of teak tree from a forest area. The applicants were charged under various sections of Indian Forest Act, Wild Life Protection Act, Prevention of Damages to Public Property Act, M.P. Vanopaj (Vyapar) Viniyam Adhiniyam, and Jaiv Vividhata Adhiniyam. The court allowed the bail application on strict terms and ordered the applicants to comply with certain conditions, including a personal bond of Rs. 1,00,000 each and not contacting any other accused persons in the case. The order will remain effective until the end of the trial.
