The court has granted bail to Ravindra Singh Gurjar, who was accused of transporting sand from a prohibited area of the Chambal river along with co-accused Jogendra. The act of transporting sand from the prohibited area is a violation of the Wild Life Protection Act and the Indian Forest Act. The applicant had been in custody since May 29, 2017, and a co-accused had already been granted bail. The court directed the concerned court to release the applicant on bail upon furnishing a personal bond and a solvent surety of Rs. 30,000 each. The applicant must abide by the conditions enumerated in Section 437(3) of the Cr.P.C.
