In a criminal case (No. 352 of 2017) involving the theft of gold jewelry from a house, the police have identified the involvement of one Prabakaran in the theft. Prabakaran has confessed to giving 20 sovereigns of gold jewelry to his wife Radhika, who in turn has pledged the same with Muthoot Fincorp Limited, Chennai. The police have issued a notice under Section 91 Cr.P.C to the Manager of Muthoot Fincorp Limited, Chennai, to appear before them or face charges under Section 411 IPC.

The court has directed Muthoot Fincorp Limited to cooperate with the police for the investigation but cannot be prosecuted under Section 411 IPC. This court order is significant as it highlights the responsibility of financial institutions in preventing and reporting criminal activities.

Theft is a serious crime, and the involvement of financial institutions in such cases can have far-reaching consequences.
