The petitioner had filed an application under Section 91 Cr.P.C. for summoning a letter dated 16.09.2009 issued by the Additional Chief Engineer, P.H.E.D., Jaipur. However, the trial court dismissed the application, prompting the petitioner to file a petition under Section 482 Cr.P.C. to challenge the order.

The High Court, in its decision, upheld the trial court's decision and dismissed the petition. This means that the petitioner can still seek to have the document summoned, but must do so at a later stage in the trial.

The court's decision emphasizes that the trial court is the appropriate forum for addressing issues related to the summoning of documents, and that such issues should be addressed at the appropriate stage of the trial.
