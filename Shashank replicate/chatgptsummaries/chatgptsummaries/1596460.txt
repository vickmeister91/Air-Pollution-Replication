The court order in question pertains to a writ appeal against the order of dismissal passed against the respondent. The learned Single Judge had set aside the order of dismissal on the ground that the report of the Enquiry Officer was furnished to the respondent along with the show cause notice and before taking a decision with regard to punishment no opportunity was given to the writ petitioner. The court referred to various judgments of the Apex Court and held that the non-issuance of the second show cause notice by furnishing the enquiry officer's report would not by itself be a ground for setting aside the order of dismissal. The impugned order of the learned single Judge was set aside and the order of termination was restored.
