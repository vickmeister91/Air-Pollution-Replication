The Patna High Court has granted anticipatory bail to Birendra Singh and Indrajeet Singh in connection with a case filed against them under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Mines Act, and Prevention of Pollution Act. The case was filed after 35 tractors loaded with sand were found at Kochar Balu Ghat under Barun P.S. The petitioners were apprehensive of their arrest in the case, and the court allowed their bail on the condition that they surrender or get arrested within six weeks and furnish a bail bond of Rs.10,000 each with two sureties. The bailors should be local and have sufficient immovable property within the jurisdiction of the court. The petitioners must cooperate in the trial and be present on every date fixed by the court. If they tamper with evidence or witnesses, the prosecution can move for cancellation of their bail.
