The court order in question pertains to a writ petition filed by V.S. Gunaseelan against the Forest Range Officer, Pollachi Forest Range, Aanamalai Tiger Reserve Forest, Pollachi Taluk, Coimbatore District. The petitioner had requested a writ of Mandamus directing the respondent to conduct a thorough investigation in respect of seizure of property in connection with WL.O.R.No.3/2014 dated 01.07.2014, on the file of respondent. 

It is important to note that the petitioner had earlier filed a petition in Crl.O.P.No.5118 of 2015 challenging the action taken against him under Section 51 (1) of Wild Life Protection Act, 1972. The court had directed the respondent to conduct a thorough investigation and in case it is found that the petitioner had valid certificate of possession, he would be entitled to the same by inheritance. However, the petitioner had filed the writ petition as no enquiry had been conducted on the same, till date.

The court, in its order, directed the respondent to conduct an investigation in the light of the order passed by this Court in Crl.O.P.No.5118 of 2015 dated 05.12.016 and conclude the same as expeditiously as possible and in any event, the investigation shall not extend beyond a period of four weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
