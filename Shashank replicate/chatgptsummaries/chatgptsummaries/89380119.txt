Mahesh Gundecha has filed an anticipatory bail application in connection with Crime No. 0102/2019 registered with Parner Police Station, Taluka Parner, District Ahmednagar, for the offences punishable under Sections 379, 427 of the Indian Penal Code and Section 15 of the Environment Protection Act. The court has directed the applicant to attend the Parner Police Station, District Ahmednagar, from 1st April, 2019 to 9th April, 2019 and shall be with the Investigating Officer from 11.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m., and shall extend full co-operation to the investigating agency.
