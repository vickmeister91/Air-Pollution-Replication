The court order pertains to an anticipatory bail application filed by Sikandar Moulali Patel, who is apprehending arrest in a criminal case registered against him under Sections 379 r/w Section 34 of the Indian Penal Code and under Sections 8 and 15 of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986. The court has granted him pre-arrest bail on the condition that he furnishes a PR bond of Rs. 15,000/- with one or two local sureties in the like amount, and does not tamper with the evidence or pressurize the prosecution witnesses. The matter has been adjourned to 18th January, 2019.
