The court order in question pertains to a case where an applicant's vehicle was transporting sand from a prohibited area reserved for Sone Gadhyal Forest Century. The police registered a case against the applicant for violating various acts. The applicant filed an application under Section 457 of Cr.P.C. to take possession of the seized vehicle, which was dismissed by the court. The applicant filed a revision, which was also dismissed.

The court found that no confiscation proceedings had been initiated by the competent authorities and directed the court to enquire whether such proceedings had been initiated. If not, the court directed the release of the vehicle on "Superdaginama" to the applicant.
