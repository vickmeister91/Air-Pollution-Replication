The court order in question pertains to a case where the petitioner, Shishir Kumar Sinha, challenged the rejection of his application under Section 91 of the Code of Criminal Procedure by the Special Judge (for CBI cases), Jabalpur. Sinha had requested the call detail records of two mobile numbers seized from the possession of the complainant and two independent witnesses for effective adjudication of the case. However, the court rejected Sinha's application, stating that the trial court has the discretion to summon documents and that Sinha can requisition his call details from his service provider. As a result, the petition was dismissed.
