The court has granted anticipatory bail to Annu Khan in connection with a forest crime case registered at Police Station Forest Range Dhamokhar, District Umariya (M.P.). The court has directed that in the event of arrest, Annu Khan shall be released on bail on furnishing a personal bond of Rs.25,000/- with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the Arresting Police Officer.
