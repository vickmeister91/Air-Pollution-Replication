The court order pertains to a criminal revision filed by the petitioner, Siva, seeking to set aside the order of the learned Judicial Magistrate No.II, Chengalpattu, Kancheepuram District, dismissing the petition to return his Bajaj Motor Cycle bearing Registration No. TN-07-CL-7968. The vehicle was seized by the respondent police in connection with a case registered under the Wild Life Forest Act, 1972.

The court granted interim custody of the vehicle to the petitioner subject to certain conditions, including executing a bond for Rs. 25,000 and filing an affidavit of undertaking not to dispose or alter the physical features of the vehicle. The RC book of the vehicle was to be retained by the Trial Court until the disposal of the trial.

The court order is related to case number Crl.R.C.No.936 of 2019 and is addressed to the Judicial Magistrate No.II, Chengalpattu, Kancheepuram District, the Forest Range Officer, Thiruporur Forest Range, Kancheepuram District, and the Public Prosecutor, High Court, Chennai. The date of the order is September 19, 2019. The order does not specify the details of the case or the reason for the communication.
