The court order in question pertains to a criminal revision case between Sh. Utpal Dey and M/S. Barclays Bank Plc. The revision was filed against an order dated 25.03.2011, which dismissed an application under section 91 Cr.P.C. by the revisionist. The court has ordered that copies of the documents executed at the time of the loan and statement of account maintained by the respondent be supplied to the revisionist within a month to enable effective cross-examination of the respondent's witnesses. The trial court is directed to fix the date for respondent/complainant's evidence after a month. The revision petition stands disposed of.
