The court has granted anticipatory bail to Sunil Kumar Mishra, who was apprehended for his arrest in connection with a case registered under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, Jharkhand Minor Minerals Concession Rules, 2004, and MMRD Act, 1957. The prosecution case relates to illegal crushing of stone chips by the owner of the factory and loading of the stone chips on the trucks seized. The court granted bail to the petitioner on furnishing a bail bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned CJM, Khunti, subject to the conditions laid down under section 438(2) Cr.P.C.
