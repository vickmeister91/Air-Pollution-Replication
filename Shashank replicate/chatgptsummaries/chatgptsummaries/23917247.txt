Anticipatory bail is a provision under the Criminal Procedure Code, which allows a person to seek bail in anticipation of his arrest. This provision is available to a person who apprehends arrest in a non-bailable offense. The grant of anticipatory bail is subject to certain conditions, which are enumerated in sub-Section (2) of Section 438 of Cr.P.C.

One of the conditions for the grant of anticipatory bail is that the applicant must make himself available for interrogation by a police officer as and when required.
