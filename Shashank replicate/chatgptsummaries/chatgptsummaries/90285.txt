The Kerala High Court recently quashed a complaint filed against K.L. Cyril under the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act. The court found that the complaint was not maintainable because it was not laid by a person authorized to do so under Section 40 of the Act. The court also noted that the Chief Environmental Engineer was the only person authorized to lay the complaint, and that the Assistant Environmental Engineer who filed the complaint was not authorized to do so. As a result, the court allowed the petition and quashed the proceedings against the petitioner.
