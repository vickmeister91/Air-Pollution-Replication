The Ramgarh Wildlife Sanctuary is located in the district of Bundi in Rajasthan. It is spread over an area of 252.79 square kilometers and is home to a variety of flora and fauna. The sanctuary was established in 1982 with the aim of protecting the wildlife and their habitats. The sanctuary is an important part of the ecosystem of the region and is a major tourist attraction.

The issue at hand is related to the alteration of the boundaries of the sanctuary. The Collector of Bundi had issued orders altering the boundaries of the sanctuary, which were challenged by the State of Rajasthan and others.
