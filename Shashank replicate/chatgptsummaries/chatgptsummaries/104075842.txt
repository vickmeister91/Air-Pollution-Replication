The court order in question pertains to a bail application under Section 439 of the Cr.P.C. for the offence punishable under Sections 39(3), (A)/51, 48-A/51, 9/51, 52/51 of the Wild Life Protection Act. The order also lists the conditions that the applicants must comply with.
