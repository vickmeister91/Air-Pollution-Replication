The Jharkhand State Pollution Control Board (JSPCB) has been ordered by the court to allow S.P.N. Minerals to continue with its crushing activities until the next date of hearing. The petitioner argues that the order was passed without following the principles of natural justice and without complying with the procedure laid down under Section 21(4) of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.
