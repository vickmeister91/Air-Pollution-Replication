The Learned Trial Court found the evidence convincing and convicted the appellant for an offense punishable under the Motor Vehicles Act.

The appellant argued that the prosecution case completely rested on the testimony of police witnesses who were interested witnesses, and no independent public witness was joined at the time the appellant was challaned for alleged violation of traffic rules. The court dismissed the appeal and upheld the conviction.
