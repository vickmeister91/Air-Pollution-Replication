The court order in question pertains to a criminal revision petition filed by four accused persons seeking to set aside an order passed by the II Additional District and Sessions Judge, Kodagu-Madikeri, sitting at Virajpet, in a matter bearing Sessions Case No. 56/2016. The accused persons were charged with offences punishable under Sections 302, 323, 324, 506 (2) & 120(B) IPC read with Sections 9, 27, 31 r/w Section 51 of the Wild Life Protection Act, 1972 and Section 25(1B)(a) of Arms Act, 1959.

The court dismissed the petition and upheld the order passed by the Sessions Judge. However, the court clarified that the order passed by the Sessions Judge or confirmation thereof by the court would not come in the way of the accused persons seeking discharge of the offences alleged against them under the Wild Life Protection Act, 1972, if they are so advised.
