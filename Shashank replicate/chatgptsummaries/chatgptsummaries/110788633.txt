These conditions include that the accused shall make themselves available for interrogation by a police officer as and when required, shall not, directly or indirectly, make any inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the facts of the case so as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts to the court or to any police officer, and shall not leave India without the previous permission of the court.
