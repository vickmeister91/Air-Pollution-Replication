The Allahabad High Court recently upheld the orders passed by the U.P. Water Pollution Prevention and Control Board and the Cess Appellate Authority, which assessed and levied Cess upon the Municipal Board, Bareilly under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act 1977 for the water it supplies to residents for domestic purposes. The court held that the levy of Cess on Municipal Boards for supplying water for domestic purposes is valid and has a nexus with the prevention and control of water pollution.

The court also noted that it may need to be decided whether Municipal Boards are authorized to pass on the Cess to consumers. The writ petition was dismissed.
