The court order in question pertains to a Criminal Original Petition that was filed under Section 482 of the Criminal Procedure Code (Cr.P.C.). The petitioner had challenged an order that was made on 18th June 2018 in C.R.P.No.64 of 2017 on the file of the learned Third Additional District and Sessions Court, Erode at Gobichettipalayam. This order confirmed the order that was made on 3rd November 2017 in C.M.P.No.2220 of 2017 in STC.No.4764 of 2014 on the file of the learned Judicial Magistrate No.I, Gobichettipalayam.

The accused had filed CMP.No.2220 of 2017 in STC.No.4764 of 2014 under Section 91 of the Cr.P.C. This was done in order to seek a direction to the complainant to produce seven documents. However, the trial court had dismissed CMP.No.2220 of 2017 in STC.No.4764 of 2014 on 3rd November 2017. The accused had then filed C.R.P.No.64 of 2017 before the Sessions Court. However, the same was dismissed by the III Additional District and Sessions Judge, Erode, Gobichettypalayam on 18th June 2018.

The present petition was filed in order to challenge the orders that were passed by the Courts below. However, the court dismissed the petition as being devoid of merits.

In summary, the court order pertains to a criminal case where the accused had filed a petition seeking the production of certain documents. However, the trial court had dismissed the petition and the accused had then filed a revision petition before the Sessions Court. This petition was also dismissed and the present petition was filed to challenge the orders passed by the Courts below. However, the court dismissed the petition as being devoid of merits.
