Sunil Markli, who was accused of illegally transporting sand, has been granted anticipatory bail by the court. The court took note of the fact that Markli had already submitted an explanation in response to a notice from the Tahsildar, but the complaint was lodged before his explanation was considered. The court has ordered Markli to be released on a personal bond of Rs. 5,000 with one surety of the same amount. However, the court has imposed certain conditions on Markli, including that he not tamper with prosecution evidence and attend the police station once a week until the charge sheet is filed. The matter has been adjourned to January 22, 2019.
