Phool Singh Banjara had filed a bail application under Section 438 of Cr.P.C. for anticipatory bail in connection with Crime No.23/2010 registered at Police Station Maksudangadh, District Guna, for the offence punishable under Sections 9 and 51 of Wild Life Protection Act. The prosecution had alleged that some skin and meat of black buck along with a plastic bag were found in the field of the applicant. The court allowed the bail application and directed that in the event of arrest, the applicant shall be enlarged on bail on his furnishing a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with a surety bond in the like amount to the satisfaction of Arresting officer/competent Court.
