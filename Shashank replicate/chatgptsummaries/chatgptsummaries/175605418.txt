The court, however, found no illegality or perversity in the impugned order passed by the trial court and dismissed Bharadwaj's application under Section 482 of the Cr.P.C.

Section 311, on the other hand, empowers a court to summon any person as a witness or to recall and re-examine any person already examined if it appears to the court that such person's evidence is necessary for the just decision of the case.

In the present case, Bharadwaj had sought to use these provisions to bring the entire accounts of the respondent before the court and to have an opportunity to recross-examine the complainant. However, the trial court had dismissed his application, and the higher court had found no reason to set aside this order.
