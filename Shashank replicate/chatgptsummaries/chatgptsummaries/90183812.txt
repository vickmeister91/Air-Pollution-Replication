On 20th September 2017, the Madras High Court passed an order in a criminal original petition filed by D. The court heard both parties and after considering the arguments put forth, set aside the order passed by the Chief Metropolitan Magistrate. The court directed the Magistrate to summon the documents listed in the petition within a week. The criminal original petition was disposed of by the court.
