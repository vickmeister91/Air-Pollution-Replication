In 2007, Mohsin Ali was convicted by the Trial Court for unlawful trade of wild birds and was sentenced to three years of rigorous imprisonment and a fine of Rs. 25,000. In 2013, he filed a criminal appeal against the judgment on the grounds that he had already completed his sentence and was unable to pay the fine due to financial constraints. However, the court dismissed the appeal, stating that a previous appeal had already been allowed in favor of the appellant in 2008, and a fresh appeal was not maintainable.
