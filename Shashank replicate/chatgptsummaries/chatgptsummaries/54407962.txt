The court has granted anticipatory bail to Mahabir Mahto and three others in connection with a case registered under sections of the Indian Forest Act and the Wild Life Protection Act. The petitioners have agreed to deposit Rs. 5000 each with the Forest Range Officer and undertake not to go over or near the place of occurrence land during the pendency of the case.
