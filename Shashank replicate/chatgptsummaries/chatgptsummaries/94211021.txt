The court order pertains to a modification of a previous order dated 10.10.2018 in a case related to the Indian Forest Act and Wild Life Protection Act. The court received a letter stating that the previous order was not related to the court of learned S.D.J.M, Pakur. As a result, the order is modified to reflect that the pending court is the court of learned S.D.J.M, Latehar. The petitioners are directed to surrender before the court within four weeks and furnish their bail bonds. A copy of the order is to be communicated to the court of learned S.D.J.M, Latehar through FAX.
