The case in question involves a petition filed by the applicants under Section 482 of the Criminal Procedure Code (Cr.P.C.) to challenge an order passed by the Special Judge (NDPS Act) Jaora, District Ratlam in Special Sessions Trial No.19/2017. The order dismissed the applicants' application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. read with Section 65 (B) of the Evidence Act. The applicants alleged that police officers had forcefully taken them from Jaora bus stand and prepared a false case against them. They requested the call details of the police officers' mobile numbers to prove their location at the time of the incident.

The court allowed the application and directed the trial judge to send an intimation to the telecom company to preserve the record of the calls made by and on the mobile numbers mentioned above, as well as their locations at the relevant point of time. The petition was disposed of accordingly.
