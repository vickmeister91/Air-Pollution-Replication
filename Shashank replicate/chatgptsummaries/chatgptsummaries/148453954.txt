The Patna High Court recently granted pre-arrest bail to Saroj Kumar Singh in connection with a case registered under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Minor Mineral Concession Rule, and Pollution Act. The petitioner argued that he cannot be held responsible for carrying sand without challan and GPS as he is only the owner of the tractor which was plying on hire. The court directed the petitioner to be released on bail on furnishing a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Aurangabad, subject to the conditions laid down under Section 438(2) Cr. P.C.
