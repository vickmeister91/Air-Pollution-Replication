The Rajasthan High Court recently issued a court order in a civil writ petition filed by Rajasthan Fort and Palace Pvt. Ltd. against the State of Rajasthan and other respondents. The petitioner had applied for environmental clearance, but the Letter of Intent granted to them was cancelled by the respondents. The court directed the petitioner to approach the Revisional Authority as per the Rajasthan Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 2017. The Revisional Authority was directed to decide on the matter within four months. The court also stated that the pending application for environmental clearance would be considered as if the order had been put under abeyance. The writ petition was disposed of with these observations.
