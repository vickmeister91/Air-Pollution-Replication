The Bombay High Court recently issued a court order regarding the violations of the Wastes (Management & Transboundary) Rules, 2016 by the General Manager of Nice Paper Mill. The court found prima facie evidence of violations and ordered the respondent to rectify the defects and remove the violations within two months. However, the court also emphasized that the respondent must remain bound to the law and relevant provisions.

The Wastes (Management & Transboundary) Rules, 2016 were introduced by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change to regulate the management of hazardous and other wastes in India. The rules aim to ensure the safe handling, storage, and disposal of wastes to protect human health and the environment.
