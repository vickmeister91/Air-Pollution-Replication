The court order in question pertains to a writ petition filed by Paul Varghese, proprietor of Pullan Granites, challenging the refusal of consent sought by him as per Ext.P5 order. The court dismissed the writ petition, stating that Ext.P5 is an order appealable under Section 28 of the Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act and Section 3 of AIR (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, and that an effective alternate remedy is available to the petitioner. However, the court directed that if the petitioner files an appeal against Ext.P5 before the appellate authority within 4 weeks from the date of the order, the appellate authority shall entertain the appeal and deal with the same on merits.
