The case of Pattimurugan, who was arrested for elephant poaching, has been the subject of a Criminal Original Petition filed by the petitioner himself. The petitioner was granted statutory bail under Section 167(2) Cr.P.C. on condition he furnish a bond for Rs.1,00,000/- with title documents.
