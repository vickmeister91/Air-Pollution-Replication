The Kerala High Court recently heard a writ petition filed by a petitioner requesting a writ of mandamus or any other appropriate writ, order or direction commanding the respondents to take effective measures to prevent the nuisance, sound and noise pollution by using high volume loudspeakers and sound systems by the 5th respondent and her henchmen in Vallam area continuing without any permission or license from the statutory authorities.

The petitioner had alleged that the 5th respondent and her associates were using high volume loudspeakers and sound systems in the Vallam area without any permission or license from the statutory authorities, causing a great deal of nuisance, sound and noise pollution. The petitioner had also alleged that despite several complaints to the authorities, no action had been taken to prevent the same.

The court directed the 1st respondent to consider and pass appropriate orders on the complaint taking necessary steps in accordance with the law, after giving an opportunity of hearing to the petitioner and also the 5th respondent, as expeditiously as possible.

The court's decision is significant as it highlights the importance of taking effective measures to prevent noise pollution and nuisance caused by the use of high volume loudspeakers and sound systems. The court's direction to the 1st respondent to consider and pass appropriate orders on the complaint is a step in the right direction towards ensuring that such activities are curbed and the rights of citizens to a peaceful and healthy environment are protected.

The court's decision also underscores the need for statutory authorities to take prompt and effective action in response to complaints of noise pollution and nuisance caused by the use of high volume loudspeakers and sound systems. It is important for authorities to ensure that such activities are carried out in accordance with the law and that the rights of citizens are protected.

Overall, the court's decision in this case is a positive development in the fight against noise pollution and nuisance caused by the use of high volume loudspeakers and sound systems. It is hoped that the authorities will take prompt and effective action in response to complaints of such activities and that citizens will be able to enjoy a peaceful and healthy environment free from such nuisance and pollution.
