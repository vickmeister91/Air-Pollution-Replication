The Jharkhand High Court has granted anticipatory bail to Gaurav Mundra, the General Manager of M/s. Sangeeta Sales Private Limited, who was apprehended for arrest in connection with a case filed against him under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, M.M.D.R. Act, Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, and Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act. The petitioner was accused of deviation in the handling and transportation of coal from Amrapali Project of CCL to Prayagraj in the State of Uttar Pradesh.

The court directed the petitioner to surrender in the Court of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Latehar within six weeks from the date of the order and granted bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.25,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each. The petitioner was also directed to cooperate with the investigation of the case and ensure that no pollution is caused during the transportation.
