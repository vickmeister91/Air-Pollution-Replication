The Tamil Nadu Fly Ash Bricks and Block's Manufactures Association filed a writ petition against the Union of India and various thermal power stations. The petitioner sought a writ of Mandamus directing the respondents to implement an Office Memorandum dated 18.3.2011, issued by the first respondent and forbearing from collecting any service charge from the member of the petitioner-Association in respect of the Fly Ash allotted to the said Members. The court found that the issue involved in the writ petitions was covered by the decision rendered by a Division Bench of the court in a previous case and allowed the writ petitions on the same lines.
