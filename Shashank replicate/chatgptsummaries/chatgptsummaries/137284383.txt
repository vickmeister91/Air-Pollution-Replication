The Karnataka State Pollution Control Board filed a case against M/s Puravankara Projects Ltd for constructing a residential complex without obtaining clearance from the Ministry of Environment and Forest, Government of India. The court has directed the petitioner to pay a fine of Rs. 1,00,000/- for violating the provisions of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986. The court has also noted that subsequent to the inspection, the petitioner had obtained the necessary clearance from the competent authorities.
