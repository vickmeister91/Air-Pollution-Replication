The court has granted bail to Ramsingh and Ramkishor, who were in custody since 7th December 2016, in connection with a case registered at Police Station Bharveli, District Balaghat for the offence under Sections 39, 44, 51 and 52 of the Wild Life Protection Act. The applicants were accused of possessing two horns of swamp deer illegally. The court allowed the bail application without commenting on the merits of the case and ordered the applicants to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 30,000 each with a solvent surety each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.
