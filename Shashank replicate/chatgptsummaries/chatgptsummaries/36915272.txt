The court order pertains to a bail application filed by Anis Alias Dandiyo Safibhai Sheikh under Section 439 of the Code of Criminal Procedure for regular bail in connection with an FIR registered against him for the offences punishable under Sections 65(A) (E), 98 and 99 of the Gujarat Prohibition Act. The applicant was accused of hiding liquor in an auto rickshaw, which was found nearby the place of Mr. Javed @ Jhon, and upon search of the said rickshaw, 767 bottles of illegal liquor were found.

The court allowed the application and ordered the applicant to be released on bail on executing a bond of Rs.10,000/- with certain conditions. The competent authority will release the applicant only if the applicant is not required in connection with any other offence for the time being.
