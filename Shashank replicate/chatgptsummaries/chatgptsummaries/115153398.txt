The Patna High Court has granted bail to Atiqur Rahman and Ajazur Rahman, who were apprehending arrest in connection with a case in which cognizance has been taken for the offence punishable under Section 15 of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 as well as section 44 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act. The court ordered that the petitioners be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate (Spl. J.M.), Patna in connection with Complaint Case No. 7(M) of 2005 subject to certain conditions.

The case in question pertains to the violation of environmental laws and pollution control regulations. The accused, Atiqur Rahman and Ajazur Rahman, were apprehending arrest in connection with the case. However, the Patna High Court has granted them bail on certain conditions.

J.M.), Patna in connection with Complaint Case No. 7(M) of 2005. The bail has been granted subject to certain conditions, which the accused must comply with.
