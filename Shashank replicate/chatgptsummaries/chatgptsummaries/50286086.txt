The court order in question pertains to a revision filed by the petitioner against an order of the Special Judge, NDPS, Neemuch in SST No.47/2017. The petitioner was found in possession of 35 kg of opium without a valid license. The petitioner had filed an application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. and Section 65 of I.T. Act, seeking preservation of call details of certain mobile numbers related to police personnel who were members of the trap party. The trial judge had dismissed the application, but the court partially allowed the petition and directed the trial judge to send intimation to the concerning company to preserve the record of calls made by and on the mentioned mobile numbers and their locations at the relevant point of time.
