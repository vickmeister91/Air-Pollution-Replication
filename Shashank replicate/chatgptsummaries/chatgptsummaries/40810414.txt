Section 91 of the Criminal Procedure Code (Cr.P.C) empowers a court to issue a summons or order to produce any document or thing that is necessary for the purposes of any investigation, inquiry, trial or other proceeding under the Code. The section also allows the court to order any person in possession of such a document or thing to produce it before the court.
