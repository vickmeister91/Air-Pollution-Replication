The court order in question pertains to a case where the petitioner has challenged the order passed by the Additional Sessions Judge disallowing his application under Section 91 of the Cr.P.C. The petitioner had requested the court to call the call details of the deceased's mobile phone and the statements recorded during the merg inquest. The trial court dismissed the application stating that the call details and statements were not part of the prosecution story. The court observed that the trial is at an initial stage and if the petitioner deems fit, he may obtain the call details and copies of statements and file them before the trial court. The petition was dismissed.
