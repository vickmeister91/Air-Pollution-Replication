The court order in question pertains to a petition filed by Himatlal Muljibhai Talati under Section 17(1)(a)(i) of CrPC. The petitioner, who was a Professional Director of Gujarat Distilleries (India) Ltd., contended that he was not involved in the day-to-day affairs of the company and, therefore, could not have been prosecuted.

The court allowed the petition and quashed the complaint and process issued against the petitioner. The court also directed the concerned Magistrate to expedite the hearing of the criminal case against the other accused. This decision was based on the fact that the petitioner was not involved in the day-to-day affairs of the company and, therefore, could not have been held responsible for any violations of the Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974.
