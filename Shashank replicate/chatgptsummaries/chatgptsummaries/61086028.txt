In a recent court case, a revision under Section 397 read with Section 401 of Cr.P.C. was dismissed by the court. The petitioner/accused was facing trial under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act before JMFC, Rewa. The petitioner had filed an application under Section 91 of the Cr.P.C. for calling the details of a cheque and an account, which were both dismissed by the learned JMFC. The court found no irregularity, illegality or perversity in the order impugned and therefore dismissed the revision.
