The court order in question pertains to a petition filed by a petitioner seeking a stay on the execution of an order passed by the Geologist, Bhuj-Kutch. The court noted that the Apex Court had already imposed restrictions on mining activities in the area covered under the Act. Therefore, the court found no reason to issue a writ and rejected the petition with no order as to costs.
