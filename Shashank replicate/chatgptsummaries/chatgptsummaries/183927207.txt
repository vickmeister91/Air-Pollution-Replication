The court has granted bail to Rahul Singh Parmar, who had been in custody since November 2015 in connection with a criminal complaint case registered at P.S. Amanganj, District Panna for offences punishable under Sections 2,9,39, 50 of the Wild Life Protection Act. The court allowed the bail application, considering the fact that the applicant had been in custody for about 4.5 months and that the investigation had been completed and the challan filed. The applicant was directed to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 30,000/- with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of JMFC concerned or CJM for his appearance in the trial court on the dates so fixed by that court during the trial.
