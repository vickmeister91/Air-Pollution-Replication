The court order pertains to a case filed by Col. R.K. Sareen (Retd.) against Wg. Cdr. Vikas Sareen and others, challenging the dismissal of his application under Section 156(3) of the Cr.P.C. The complainant alleged that a will had been forged and fabricated in relation to his mother's properties, and that one of the properties had been sold on the basis of the forged will. The court heard arguments from both sides and ultimately dismissed the revision petition, stating that the complainant could proceed with an application under Section 200 of the Cr.P.C. or file a separate complaint. The court also noted that the complainant had not filed a detailed affidavit in support of his application under Section 156(3) of the Cr.P.C.
