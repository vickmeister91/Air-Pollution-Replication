The court order in question pertains to a case where the petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection with a case registered under Sections 26, 41, 52, 64 of the Indian Forest Act and Section 27/29 of the Wild Life Protection Act. The petitioner has been directed to surrender in the Court within four weeks and in the event of his arrest or surrender, he shall be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Koderma in connection with G. Case No. 153 of 2015, subject to certain conditions.
