The court recently rejected the bail application of Surajlal, also known as Guddu, who had been in custody since February 2, 2015. Surajlal was charged with violating several sections of the Wild Life Protection Act, 1972, including Section 2 Sub-section (I)(20)(36), Section 48(A), 49A(a), 49(B), Section 50, and 51.
