The court order pertains to a criminal case where the accused has filed a petition under Section 482 of the Cr.P.C. to challenge two orders passed by the trial court. The court has heard both parties and perused the impugned orders to decide on the matter.

The court order states that while an accused has the right to request the summoning of documents, the court has the right to examine the relevance of the documents and determine if they are necessary for the trial.
