The court recently granted bail to Yogendra Singh, who had been in custody since January 28, 2018, in connection with Forest Crime No.7376/2018, registered at Police Station Forest Range Ghunghuti, District-Umariya (M.P.). Singh was charged with offences punishable under Sections 2(16), 2(31), 9, 39, 44, 48, 50, 51 Section 52 of the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972. The court allowed an application for taking additional documents on record in a bail application filed by Singh under Section 439 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.

The court granted bail to Singh on the condition that he furnish a bail bond of Rs. 40,000 with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the JMFC concerned or the trial Court for his appearance before it on the dates given by the concerned Court. This decision was made after considering the facts and circumstances of the case, as well as the arguments put forth by both the prosecution and the defence.
