The court order in question pertains to a criminal original petition filed by K. Mareeswaran against the Inspector of Police, Kalligudi Police Station, Virudhunagar District. The petitioner had filed an application under Section 91 of the Criminal Procedure Code for production of certain documents, which was dismissed by the learned Judicial Magistrate, Thirumangalam, on the ground that he had earlier taken out an application for the same relief and that was allowed. However, the petitioner argued that the documents summoned earlier did not contain the relevant particulars and sought production of additional documents to prove his innocence. The court allowed the petition to secure the ends of justice and set aside the earlier order.
