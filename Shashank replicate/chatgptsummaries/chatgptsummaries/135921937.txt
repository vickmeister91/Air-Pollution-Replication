The court order in question pertains to two petitions that have been filed in relation to the acquisition of forest land for coal mining in the Pakri-Barwadih area of Hazaribagh district in Jharkhand. The petitioners have alleged violations of the Forest Conservation Act, 1980, and the Scheduled Tribes and Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006.

The court has allowed the petitioners to implead the Ministry of Forest, Environment and Climate Change, Government of India as Respondent No. 14 in one of the petitions.
