In a recent court order, the case between Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd. and Madhya Pradesh Biodiversity was addressed. The notice issued by the Biodiversity Board under Section 7 of the Biological Diversity Act, 2002 is going to be withdrawn, as stated by the respondents' counsel. This notice had caused concern for Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd., as it pertained to the use of certain plant species in their products. The withdrawal of the notice is a positive development for the company, as it removes the threat of legal action.

The petitioners, Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd., sought permission to withdraw their petitions with liberty to file afresh if needed. The court granted this prayer, allowing the petitions to be dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to the petitioners to take recourse of law afresh if needed. This means that Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd. can pursue legal action again in the future if necessary.

The Biological Diversity Act, 2002 is an important piece of legislation in India that seeks to conserve biological diversity, promote sustainable use of its components, and ensure fair and equitable sharing of benefits arising from the use of biological resources. The Act establishes Biodiversity Management Committees at the local level to oversee the conservation and sustainable use of biological resources. It also provides for the establishment of Biodiversity Boards at the state and national levels to regulate access to biological resources and ensure compliance with the Act.

In this case, the notice issued by the Madhya Pradesh Biodiversity Board under Section 7 of the Act had caused concern for Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd. The notice pertained to the use of certain plant species in their products, and the company was worried about the legal implications of continuing to use these species. However, with the withdrawal of the notice, the company can continue to use these species without fear of legal action.

The court order granting the petitioners permission to withdraw their petitions with liberty to file afresh if needed is also significant. It provides a safety net for Bajrang Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd. in case they need to pursue legal action again in the future. This is important because the use of biological resources is a complex issue, and companies need to be able to navigate the legal landscape effectively to ensure compliance with the law.
