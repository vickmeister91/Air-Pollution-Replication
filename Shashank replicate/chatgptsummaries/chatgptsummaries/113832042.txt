The court recently rejected the bail application of Gammbhir Jat and others under Section 438 of Cr.P.C. The applicants claimed that they were falsely implicated in the case and that they were released on bail by the police. However, the prosecution filed an application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. along with medical documents for adding more sections, and they had apprehension of their arrest.
