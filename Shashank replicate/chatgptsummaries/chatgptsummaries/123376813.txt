The court recently granted bail to Sher Singh under Section 439 of Cr.P.C. for an offense under Sections 2, 9, 39 44, 48(ka),50, 51, 52 of Wild Life(Protection) Act, 1972 in connection with Crime No. 39007/2012. The court noted that the allegations against Sher Singh were identical to those of a co-accused who was granted bail earlier. The court directed Sher Singh to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 50,000/- with surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.

The case against Sher Singh pertains to the violation of various sections of the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972. The Act was enacted to provide for the protection of wild animals, birds, and plants, and for matters connected therewith or ancillary or incidental thereto. The Act aims to ensure the protection of wildlife and their habitats, and to prevent their exploitation for commercial purposes.
