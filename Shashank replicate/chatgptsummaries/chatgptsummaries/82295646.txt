The court order pertains to a writ petition filed by M/s Multiwal Duplex Pvt. Ltd. challenging a notice issued under Section 33-A of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. The notice directed the company to close down all manufacturing operations due to non-compliance with prescribed discharge norms and standards. The court found that the company was violating the norms and standards, resulting in a threat to the environment. The court also noted that the company has an alternative remedy as the impugned order is appealable under Section 16(c) of the National Green Tribunal Act, 2010.

The court order states that the petitioner was found to be violating environmental norms and standards, resulting in a threat to the environment. The petitioner was given a show cause notice and responded to it, but their response was not considered while passing the impugned order. The court quashes the impugned order and directs the second respondent to consider the petitioner's response and take a decision in accordance with the law within 10 days. The petitioner cannot operate until they receive consent to operate. The court issues a certified copy of the judgment.
