During the trial, the respondents filed a petition under Section 91 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, asking the petitioner to produce income tax returns for the years 2006-2007 and 2007-2008 pertaining to his personal and business transactions along with the particulars of PAN.

The learned Judicial Magistrate allowed the petition, directing the petitioner to produce the documents. The court allowed the petition and directed the learned Judicial Magistrate to dispose of the case within three months.

Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act deals with the dishonour of cheques for insufficiency of funds in the account of the drawer. The offence is punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to two years, or with a fine which may extend to twice the amount of the cheque, or with both.
