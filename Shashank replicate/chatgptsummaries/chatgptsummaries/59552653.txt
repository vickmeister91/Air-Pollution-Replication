The court order pertains to a bail application filed by Jyoti and Kishore, who were arrested for allegedly hunting a wild animal, specifically a bear, which is listed in Schedule-I of the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972. The applicants claimed that they were falsely implicated in the crime and did not own the land where the animal was electrocuted. After considering the facts and circumstances of the case, the court allowed the bail application and directed the applicants to furnish a personal bond of Rs.25,000/- each with one surety in the like sum to the satisfaction of the concerned trial court for their appearance as and when directed.
