The court has granted anticipatory bail to Samir Kumar Ghosh, who was apprehending his arrest in connection with a complaint case for an offense under Section 15 of the Environment Protection Act. The petitioner was the agent of a mining company and was accused of making production in the mines without getting environmental clearance and making higher production during the period of 2005-2006. The court directed that in the event of his surrender or arrest, the petitioner shall be released on bail on furnishing a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the like amount each.

The petitioner was accused of making production in the mines without getting environmental clearance and making higher production during the period of 2005-2006. The petitioner was the agent of a mining company, and the complaint was filed against him by the West Bengal Pollution Control Board.
