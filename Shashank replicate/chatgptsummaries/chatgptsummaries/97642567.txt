The court recently granted anticipatory bail to Vinay Prasad Mehta in connection with a case registered under sections 33 (1) (c) of Indian Forest Act and under Section 27/29 of Wild Life Protection Act. The court ordered that if the petitioner is arrested or surrenders within four weeks, he shall be released on bail on furnishing cash security of Rs.2000/- and on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 25,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each. The petitioner is also not allowed to go over or near the place of occurrence land during the pendency of the case.
