The court order pertains to a writ petition filed by N. Shamsul Huda, a conservator of forests, seeking a writ of prohibition or any other appropriate writ, order or direction, restraining respondents 3 to 10 from causing any developmental activity in the Beypore Thope Reserve Forest, in violation of the provisions of the CRZ Regulations and the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The main contention urged by the petitioner is that respondents 3 to 7 are attempting to convert the reserve forest area for the purpose of an Eco Tourism Project, which amounts to a violation of the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and Coastal Regulation Zone notification. The court observed that no activities, which are in violation of the provisions of the Act shall be undertaken, unless permission is obtained from the Central Government, in terms of the provisions of law.
