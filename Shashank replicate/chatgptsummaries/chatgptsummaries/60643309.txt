The Patna High Court has granted pre-arrest bail to Anand Singh and Shashi Kumar in connection with a case registered for offenses under Sections 379, 411, 420/34 of I.P.C. and 4/40 of M.M.C.R. and 15 of Prevention of Pollution Act. The accusation was that they were involved in illegal mining and carrying uncovered sand loaded on a truck and tractor. The court ordered that the petitioners be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the C.J.M., Aurangabad, subject to the conditions as laid down under Section 438(2) Cr.P.C.

The case against Anand Singh and Shashi Kumar pertains to illegal mining and transportation of sand. The two were accused of carrying uncovered sand loaded on a truck and tractor, which is a violation of the Prevention of Pollution Act. The case was registered under Sections 379, 411, 420/34 of I.P.C. and 4/40 of M.M.C.R. The accused had approached the Patna High Court seeking pre-arrest bail.

The Patna High Court, after hearing the arguments of both the parties, granted pre-arrest bail to Anand Singh and Shashi Kumar. The court ordered that the petitioners be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the C.J.M., Aurangabad, subject to the conditions as laid down under Section 438(2) Cr.P.C.

The court also imposed certain conditions on the accused. The accused were directed to cooperate with the investigation and not to tamper with the evidence. They were also directed not to leave the country without the permission of the court. The court further directed the accused to appear before the investigating officer as and when required.

The court observed that the accused were not involved in any other criminal case and that they had cooperated with the investigation. The court also noted that the accused were ready to cooperate with the investigation and that they had no intention of absconding.

The court further observed that the accused were entitled to the benefit of pre-arrest bail as they had not been arrested so far. The court also noted that the accused had a good reputation in society and that they were not likely to abscond or tamper with the evidence.

In conclusion, the Patna High Court has granted pre-arrest bail to Anand Singh and Shashi Kumar in connection with a case registered for offenses under Sections 379, 411, 420/34 of I.P.C. and 4/40 of M.M.C.R. and 15 of Prevention of Pollution Act. The court has imposed certain conditions on the accused and has directed them to cooperate with the investigation. The court has also observed that the accused were entitled to the benefit of pre-arrest bail as they had not been arrested so far and that they had a good reputation in society.
