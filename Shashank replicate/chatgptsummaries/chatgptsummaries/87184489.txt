The court took note of this and dismissed the writ petition, but allowed the petitioner to pursue the matter if they satisfy all the requirements.

The rules require that any person seeking to import or export hazardous wastes must obtain a license from the State Pollution Control Board. The license is granted only after the applicant satisfies certain conditions, including the provision of necessary documents and compliance with the rules.
