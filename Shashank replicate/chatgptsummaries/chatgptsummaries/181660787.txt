Section 31-A of the Act empowers the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) to issue directions to any person or authority to take measures for the prevention, control, or abatement of air pollution.

The National Green Tribunal (NGT) is a specialized court established in India for the effective and expeditious disposal of cases relating to environmental protection and conservation of forests and other natural resources. The NGT has the power to hear all civil cases relating to environmental issues and to issue orders for the prevention and control of environmental pollution.
