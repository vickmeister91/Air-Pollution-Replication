The Jammu & Kashmir High Court recently issued an order in the case of Anuradha Singh vs J&K SPCB and others on December 20, 2017. The petitioner had requested the court to quash a notice issued by the Pollution Control Board and to command the respondents to process her request for consent under the Water and Air Acts. The court directed the competent authority to afford an opportunity of hearing to the petitioner and to pass a speaking order in accordance with the law. No coercive action shall be taken against the petitioner until the order is passed. The court did not express any opinion on the merits of the petitioner's claim. The writ petition was disposed of along with connected MP.
