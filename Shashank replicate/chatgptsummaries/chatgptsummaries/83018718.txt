The plaintiff had claimed that the defendants had illegally recovered arrears of tax with penalty amounting to Rs. 18,402 from him for a bus that he had already paid tax for. The case was heard in court, and after considering the arguments put forth by both parties, the court found in favor of the plaintiff. The court held that the plaintiff had indeed paid the tax for the bus in question and that the defendants had illegally recovered arrears of tax with penalty from him. The court also noted that the defendants had failed to provide any evidence to support their claim that the plaintiff had not registered his vehicle with RTO Ghaziabad.

In light of these findings, the court ordered the defendants to pay the plaintiff Rs. 18,402 with interest at the rate of 9% from 7.9.98 till realization of the amount. This order was a significant victory for the plaintiff, who had been wrongfully charged by the defendants.
