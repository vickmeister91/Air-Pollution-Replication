Brajesh Salil Sahu and Nitin Singh have been granted bail by the court in connection with a case registered against them and co-accused Anil Martin for hunting two deers with a licensed rifle and carrying their carcasses in a Gypsy car. The applicants have been in custody since December 12, 2016, and the charge-sheet has been filed. The court has ordered them to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 30,000 each with one surety of the same amount to the satisfaction of the trial court for securing their presence on all such dates as may be fixed by it in the course of trial of the case. They shall abide by the conditions enumerated in Section 437(3) of the Cr.P.C.
