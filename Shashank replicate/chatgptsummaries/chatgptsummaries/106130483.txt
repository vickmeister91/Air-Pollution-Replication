The court has granted anticipatory bail to Mansoor Mian in connection with a case registered under Sections 26 of Indian Forest Act, 27 and 29 of Wild Life Protection Act. The petitioner had moved the court apprehending his arrest and claimed that he had been falsely implicated in the case. The court granted bail on the condition that he surrenders within three weeks and furnishes a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the same amount each to the satisfaction of the learned CJM.
