The Patna High Court recently granted anticipatory bail to Birendra Sharma and Surendra Kumar in connection with a case registered for the offence punishable under Sections 379, 411, 420/34 of the Indian Penal Code, Section 4, 40 of Bihar Mines and Minerals Concession Rules, 1972 and Section 15 of Prevention of Pollution Act, 1986. The allegation was that the petitioners along with others were illegally lifting sand from the river without any valid challan. The court ordered their release on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Aurangabad.
