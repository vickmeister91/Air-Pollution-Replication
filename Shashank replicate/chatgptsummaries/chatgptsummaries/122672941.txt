The court order in question pertains to a criminal original petition that was filed under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. The petitioner had filed a petition under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. to direct the respondent to produce the income tax returns and the bank passbook. However, the Court below had dismissed the petition on the ground that the petitioner had sought for income tax returns for the year 2009-2012 and also bank passbook for the year 2009-2012 and the petitioner had filed this petition at the stage of defendant side witness.

The Court below had found that the respondent cannot be compelled to produce the income tax returns and bank passbook and the petitioner has to rebut the presumption based on the oral and documentary evidence which is already placed on record. The Criminal Original Petition was dismissed and the Court below was directed to complete the proceedings within a period of two months from the date of receipt of copy of this order.
