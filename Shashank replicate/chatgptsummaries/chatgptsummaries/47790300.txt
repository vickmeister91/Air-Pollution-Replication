The court order pertains to a bail application filed by Rafikbhai Allarakhabhai Metar under Section 439 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, for an offence under Sections 65(A) (E), 116(B), 66(1)(B), 81, 83, 98(2) of the Prohibition Act. The court has allowed the application and ordered the release of the applicant on regular bail on certain conditions, including executing a personal bond of Rs. 10,000 and not leaving India without prior permission of the Sessions Judge concerned. The court has also considered the law laid down by the Hon'ble Apex Court in the case of Sanjay Chandra Vs. Central Bureau of Investigation, reported in [2012] 1 SCC 40.
