The Patna High Court has granted anticipatory bail to Ugeshwar Paswan in connection with a case filed against him under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Mines and Minerals Concession Rules, 1972, and Prevention of Pollution Act, 1986. The petitioner claimed that he was the owner-cum-driver of a tractor that was not loaded with sand and was parked at the time of seizure. The court allowed anticipatory bail on the condition that the petitioner surrenders within six weeks and furnishes a bail bond of Rs.10,000 with two sureties. The petitioner must also cooperate in the trial and not tamper with evidence or witnesses.
