The petitioners had sought a direction for the Collector to decide on the genuineness of their resignations and to quash an order for re-election of eight vacant seats. The court, in its ruling, upheld the order for re-election but found that the Collector had not adjudicated on the dispute over the resignations, as required by law.

The court set aside the order for re-election and directed the Collector to consider the complaint and pass an appropriate order in accordance with the law.
