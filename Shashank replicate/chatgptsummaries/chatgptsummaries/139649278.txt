The Himachal Pradesh High Court has issued an order in a case related to the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and other environmental laws. The court has also instructed the counsel for the Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission to ascertain the factual position in this regard. The case has been listed for August 17, 2018.
