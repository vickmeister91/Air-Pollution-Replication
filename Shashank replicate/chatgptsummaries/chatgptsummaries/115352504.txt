The court order pertains to a criminal case involving the petitioner, who is the sole accused in a case involving the offence under Section 51 read with Sections 9 and 2(16)b of the Wild Life Protection Act. The petitioner is alleged to have trapped a wild swine to death on his property, but he claims to have informed the forest officials about the trap. The petitioner seeks orders under Section 482 Cr.P.C quashing the prosecution against him. However, the court is not inclined to quash the prosecution and advises the petitioner to approach the trial court for discharge at the appropriate stage. The court will frame charges only if there is some material to proceed against the petitioner.
