The Director General of Police and other police officials have filed an appeal against an order passed by a single judge of the Madras High Court in a writ petition. The respondent, a police officer, had been suspended from service and filed a writ petition challenging the suspension. The single judge allowed the writ petition and directed the appellants to post the respondent in a non-sensitive post. The appellants have challenged this order in the appeal.

The case revolves around the suspension of a police officer who had filed a writ petition challenging the suspension. The single judge had allowed the writ petition and directed the appellants to post the respondent in a non-sensitive post. However, the appellants have challenged this order in the appeal.

The court has directed the Judicial Magistrate-III, Vellore to dispose of the criminal case against the respondent within three months and the Special Court, Alagiyapandipuram to dispose of the case against the Forest Department within three months. If the cases are not disposed of within the stipulated time frame, the respondent shall be placed in any non-sensitive post.
