The Patna High Court has recently granted bail to Sanjeev Kumar Singh, who was apprehensive of his arrest in a case registered against him under the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986. The allegation against him was that he was operating a diesel generator set in violation of the provisions of the Act. The court has ordered his release on bail on furnishing a bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each.
