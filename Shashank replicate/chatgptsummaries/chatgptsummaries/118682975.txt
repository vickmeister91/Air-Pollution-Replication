The court order in question pertains to a criminal original petition filed by N. Seenivasagan against The State Rep. The court allowed the petition and directed the respondent/prosecution to furnish the copy of the document Nos.3 and 4 mentioned in the petition in Crl.M.P.No.446 of 2017. This means that the prosecution is required to provide the petitioner with copies of the documents mentioned in the petition. The documents in question are the request/report of the Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-corruption, Chennai-28 to the Sanction Authority in RC.No.198/2010/TNEB/CC-IV, dated 28.07.2010.

The petitioner had filed the petition seeking direction from the court to produce these documents as they are crucial to the ongoing trial in the Special Court under Prevention of Corruption Act, Chennai. The court has now granted the petitioner's request and directed the prosecution to provide the documents.
