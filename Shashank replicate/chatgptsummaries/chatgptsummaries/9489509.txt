The court order pertains to a bail application filed by Shakeel Mohemad Sharafatali Pathan and another person in connection with a First Information Report (FIR) registered against them under the Prohibition Act. The applicants had sought regular bail, claiming that they were innocent and had been falsely implicated. The court allowed the bail application, subject to certain conditions, including the execution of a personal bond of Rs. 10,000 each, surrendering their passports, and marking their presence before the police station on specific days. The court also warned that if any of the conditions were breached, appropriate action would be taken.
