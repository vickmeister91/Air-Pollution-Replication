The court has granted bail to Chain Singh alias Raju in a case registered under Sections 3, 9, 39, 50 & 51 of Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972. The applicant was accused of hunting a wild boar with the help of three other persons. The court allowed the bail application considering that the co-accused persons have already been released on bail, charge sheet has been filed, and the trial will take considerable time. The applicant has been ordered to furnish a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.
