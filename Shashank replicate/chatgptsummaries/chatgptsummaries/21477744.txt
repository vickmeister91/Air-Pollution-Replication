The court order pertains to an application for bail under section 439 Cr.P.C. in connection with a case pending in the Court of learned S.D.J.M., Bargarh for offences punishable under sections 379/429/120-B/413/201 of the Indian Penal Code read with section 135 of Odisha Electricity Act, 2003 and section 51 of the Wild Life Protection Act. The court has considered the submissions of both parties and has decided to release the petitioners on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.20,000/- each with two solvent sureties for the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Court in session over the matter with further terms and conditions as the learned Court may deem just and proper.

The case in question involves multiple charges under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, the Odisha Electricity Act, 2003, and the Wild Life Protection Act. The accused had applied for bail under section 439 Cr.P.C., which allows for the release of a person on bail pending trial or appeal. The court considered the submissions of both parties and decided to grant bail to the petitioners.

The court has imposed certain conditions for the grant of bail. The petitioners are required to furnish a bail bond of Rs.20,000/- each with two solvent sureties for the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Court in session over the matter. This means that the petitioners must provide a sum of Rs.20,000/- each as security for their release, and must also provide two other individuals who can act as sureties and provide the same amount as security. The court has also imposed further terms and conditions as it deems just and proper.
