The court order in question pertains to a case between Synromatics and the Baroda Municipal Corporation. The case revolves around the company's failure to meet environmental norms. The court has been presented with reports of trade effluents for the years 1994-95 and 1995-96, which show that the company is not meeting the norms set by the authorities. The Gujarat Pollution Control Board (GPCB) has recommended the closure of the unit, and a legal notice was issued in 1996.

The court has directed the company to file an affidavit and has granted permission to commence trial production till 16th July 1997, subject to certain conditions. The GPCB has been directed to take samples and submit a report of analysis within one week. The court has taken a stern view of the company's non-compliance with environmental norms and has directed the GPCB to take appropriate action.
