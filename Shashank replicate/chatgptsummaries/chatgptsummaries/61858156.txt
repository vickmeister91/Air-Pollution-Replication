The court had to determine whether the management had the right to make adhoc appointments of teachers and whether the appointed teachers were entitled to salary under the Intermediate Education Act and the U.P. High School and Intermediate College (Payment of Salaries of the Teachers and Other Employees) Act.

The court held that the management had the power to make temporary/adhoc appointments under sub-section (11) of Section 16-E of the Intermediate Act for a period not exceeding eleven months, and that the salary of such teachers would be borne by the State under the Act 1971.
