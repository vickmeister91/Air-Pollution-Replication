The court order in question pertains to a petition filed by Ajhar Khan and others under Section 482 of Cr.P.C. against an order passed by the court of 10th Additional Sessions Judge, Gwalior in S.T. No.77/2014. The petitioners had filed an application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. for getting a transcript of the CD recorded by Anil Singh, a senior news reporter of S. News Channel produced before the court for cross-examination of the witness Mahendra Singh Grewal PW-2. The court dismissed the petition as the CD had already been produced before the trial court by the petitioners themselves. However, the court ordered that the petitioners have a right to cross-examine the witness PW-2 on the basis of the original CD when the PW-2 comes before the court for getting the remaining statement recorded by the court in connection with the amended charge.
