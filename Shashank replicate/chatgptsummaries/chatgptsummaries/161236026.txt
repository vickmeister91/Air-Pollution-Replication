Ramkumar Gurjar had filed a petition under Section 482 of the Criminal Procedure Code (Cr.P.C.) against the order passed by the first Additional Sessions Judge, Jaura District Morena, dismissing his application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. to call for the report of Medical Examination Board, Morena and Gwalior relating to victim Girraj Sharma. The charge-sheet against the applicant was not dependent upon the report given by the Medical Board. The court dismissed the petition under Section 482 of the Cr.P.C. filed by the applicant Ramkumar Gurjar. However, he would be free to get the copy of that Medical Board's report under Right to Information Act or at the defence stage, he may apply to call for such a report before the trial Court.
