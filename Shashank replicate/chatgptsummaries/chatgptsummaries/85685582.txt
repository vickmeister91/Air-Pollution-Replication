The court order in question pertains to a revision petition filed by Vidya Sagar Bathla against the dismissal of his applications under Section 91 Cr.P.C. The petitioner was facing a charge under Section 7 read with Section 13 of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988. The applications were filed to bring on record call details of certain individuals and registers of jamabandi/mutation and girdawari of certain villages. The trial court dismissed the applications on the ground that they were irrelevant. The petitioner did not press the plea for placing on record the call details of the phones used by certain individuals. The court found no error in the order passed by the trial court and dismissed the revision petition.
