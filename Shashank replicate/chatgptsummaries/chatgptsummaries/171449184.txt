Irshad's defense team, on the other hand, argued that he was innocent and that the allegations against him were baseless. They also pointed out that Irshad had cooperated with the investigation and had not tried to flee from the authorities.

After considering the arguments from both sides, the court ultimately decided to dismiss Irshad's petition for anticipatory bail.
