The Delhi High Court recently set aside a summoning order against M/S Inditex India Pvt Ltd & Anr for offences under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. The petitioner had applied for renewal of consent, which was granted after the complaint was filed. The court remanded the matter back to the Court of learned ACMM to consider the matter afresh, in accordance with the law. The first prayer seeking quashing of the complaint was left to be pressed at the appropriate stage.
