The Karnataka High Court recently issued a court order in response to a writ petition filed by Kempambha Granite and Robo Sand against the Government of Karnataka. The petitioners had applied for a quarrying lease, which was rejected on the grounds that the land was deemed forest land. However, the court found that no inquiry had been made to ascertain whether the land was actually forest land. Therefore, the court quashed the impugned order and directed the Deputy Director to examine whether the land is forest or forest land within the meaning of a decision of the Apex Court and the Forest Conservation Act, 1980.
