Ishak Mohammad, who was arrested by the police on 4th September 2019 for offences under Section 354, 458 & 380 of the I.P.C. and u/s. 26 & 42 of the Forest Conservation Act. The court has directed the petitioner to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 40,000 and one solvent surety in the same amount to the satisfaction of the concerned trial court.
