Section 188 of IPC deals with disobedience to an order duly promulgated by a public servant. It states that whoever disobeys an order promulgated by a public servant, which he is bound to obey, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to one month or with fine which may extend to two hundred rupees, or with both.
