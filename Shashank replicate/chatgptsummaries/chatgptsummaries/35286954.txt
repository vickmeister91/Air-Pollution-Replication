The court, while acknowledging that Section 91 Cr.P.C. empowers the court to issue summons to produce documents, observed that the said provision cannot be used as a substitute for the procedure prescribed under Section 243 Cr.P.C. The court noted that the provisions of Section 243 Cr.P.C. are mandatory and must be followed by the lower court in all cases where the accused has pleaded not guilty.

The court, while setting aside the order of the lower court, directed it to conduct a speedy trial and conclude the same expeditiously.
