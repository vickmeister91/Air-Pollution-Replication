The Madras High Court recently dismissed a writ petition filed by Great Eastern Energy Corporation Limited against the State of Tamil Nadu, Union of India, and Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board. The petitioner sought a writ of certiorarified mandamus to quash a government order and direct the first respondent to grant renewal of Petroleum Exploration Licences. However, the court held that the National Green Tribunal Act, 2010, covers environmental issues and matters, and cases should be instituted and litigated before the National Green Tribunal. The court dismissed the writ petition and directed the petitioner to approach the National Green Tribunal if it desires to seek redressal of its grievances.
