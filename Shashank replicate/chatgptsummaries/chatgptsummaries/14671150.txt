The court order in question pertains to a petition filed under Section 482 of the Cr.P.C. by Shishupal Singh against the State of Madhya Pradesh. The petitioner had filed an application under Section 457 of the Cr.P.C. for interim custody of a tractor and trolley that were seized for committing an offence under the Wild Life (Protection) Act and the Forest Act. The application was dismissed by the Chief Judicial Magistrate, District Sidhi. The petitioner challenged this order, and the court allowed the petition, setting aside the impugned order and directing the release of the offending tractor and trolley on interim custody on furnishing a Supurdginama by the petitioner in a sum of Rs. 2,50,000/- with one surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Sidhi, subject to certain terms and conditions.
