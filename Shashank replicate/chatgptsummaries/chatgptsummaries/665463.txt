The court denied anticipatory bail but allowed the petitioner to surrender before the Investigating Officer for interrogation and recovery of incriminating material. The Act aims to ensure the protection of wildlife and their habitats, as well as to regulate hunting, poaching, and trade in wildlife and their products.

Regular bail is a type of bail that is granted after an accused person has been arrested and produced before a Magistrate. The Magistrate may grant bail if they are satisfied that the accused person is not likely to abscond or interfere with the investigation.
