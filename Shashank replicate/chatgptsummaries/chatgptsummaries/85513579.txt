The court order in question pertains to a criminal petition filed by Shripad Devaru Hegde against the State. The petitioner is charged with offenses punishable under Section 408, I.P.C. The respondent made an application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. praying for the production of the statements of enquiry report. The Trial Court allowed the said summons to the Enquiry Officer and Annappa Shet. 

However, the petitioner's counsel argued that the respondent cannot maintain such an application after 12 long years. The court took note of this argument and directed the Trial Court to expedite the trial and dispose of the case within three months from the date of production of the certified enquiry report on a day-to-day basis. The petition was disposed of without setting aside the impugned order.
