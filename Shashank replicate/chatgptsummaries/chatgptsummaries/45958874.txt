The court order in question pertains to a challenge made by Gajraj Singh against an order passed by the First Additional Sessions Judge, Narsinghpur in ST No.350 of 2013. The applicant had filed an application under Section 91 of the Cr.P.C. to get call details of various persons. The trial court had dismissed the application on merits, and subsequent applications filed by other accused persons were also dismissed. The applicant did not challenge the order dated 12.9.2014, which had attained finality. The order dated 5.2.2015 is under challenge, but the court finds no illegality or perversity in the order and dismisses the criminal revision at motion stage.
