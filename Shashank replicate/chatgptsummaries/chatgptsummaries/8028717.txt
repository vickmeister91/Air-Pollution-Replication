Dinesh Sen, the applicant, filed a criminal revision questioning the rejection of his application under Section 91 of the Code of Criminal Procedure by the Judicial Magistrate First Class, Ganjbasoda, District Vidisha. The applicant favored the cheque No. 003731 dated 27.05.2014 amounting to Rs. 3,35,000 towards the payment of the loan amount. The said cheque was presented in the Bank and the same was returned unpaid with the note indicated insufficient fund. The applicant has failed to deposit the cheque amount after receiving of notice issued by the respondent.

The court dismissed the application filed by the applicant under Section 91 of the Cr.P.C. on the ground that the applicant is delaying the proceedings and those documents can be proved in defense. The revision petition was dismissed being devoid of merits.
