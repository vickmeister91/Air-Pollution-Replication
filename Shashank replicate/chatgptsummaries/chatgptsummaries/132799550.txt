The case in question involves an accusation of an offence under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act. The learned Magistrate passed an order directing the accused to produce the document. However, this order has been set aside by the court as it violates Article 20(3) of the Constitution of India.

Article 20(3) of the Constitution of India provides that no person accused of any offence shall be compelled to be a witness against himself. In this case, the order passed by the learned Magistrate directing the accused to produce the document would have compelled the accused to be a witness against himself. Therefore, the court has set aside the order as it violates Article 20(3) of the Constitution of India.

This direction has been given in order to ensure that justice is delivered in a timely manner.
