The Patna High Court has granted bail to Umesh Sah, who had been accused of running a movable brick-kiln without a license, which had already been banned. The petitioner had been in jail custody since June 22, 2016, and the application of the Indian Penal Code in the case appeared to be doubtful. The court directed the petitioner to be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Gaya, in connection with Fatehpur P.S. Case No. 37 of 2013.

The case against Umesh Sah was related to the operation of a movable brick-kiln without a license, which had been banned by the authorities.
