The Patna High Court has granted bail to Raj Kumar Singh in connection with Barun P.S. Case No. 72 of 2018. The case involves allegations of illegal sand mining and the seizure of a tractor without an E-chalan and GPS system. The court has imposed certain conditions on Singh's bail. He must surrender before the court within four weeks and furnish a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the same amount. The court has also stated that if the police call Singh for investigation and he fails to appear, the privilege of anticipatory bail will be cancelled.

The case against Raj Kumar Singh pertains to illegal sand mining in the Barun police station area. The police had seized a tractor from Singh's possession, which was allegedly used for illegal sand mining. The tractor did not have an E-chalan and GPS system, which are mandatory for vehicles used in sand mining. Singh was arrested and sent to jail in connection with the case.
