In a recent court order, Fatehkaran Mehdu filed a petition under Section 482 Cr.P.C. against the order passed by the learned Special Judge, Prevention of Corruption Act, Bhilwara in Session Case NO.02/2017. The order dismissed the application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. filed by the petitioner. The petitioner argued that the license and registration of Kishan Singh were important documents and the application under Section 91 of Cr.P.C. was relevant. The court allowed the petition and directed the learned court below to call for the necessary register/record along with the license of Kishan Singh as prayed under Section 91 of Cr.P.C.
