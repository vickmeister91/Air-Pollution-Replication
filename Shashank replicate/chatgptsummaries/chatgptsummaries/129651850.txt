Sheikh Shahjad and Gaffar Khan were arrested on January 10, 2016, for allegedly killing a black buck and possessing its meat, rifle, and cartridges. The case was brought before the court, and the applicants filed for bail under Section 439 of Cr. P. C. The senior counsel argued that the applicants had no previous criminal history and that the offence was triable by a Judicial Magistrate First Class. The court allowed the application and directed the applicants to furnish a personal bond of Rs. 50,000 each and one solvent surety in the same amount to the satisfaction of the CJM Sagar. The applicants were released on regular bail with certain conditions to be followed.
