The court has directed the lower court to decide the matter with as much expedition as possible and preferably before the end of February 2016, subject to the petitioner's appearance on all hearing dates and cooperation in the trial. This order is significant as it highlights the importance of timely disposal of cases and the need to ensure that justice is not delayed.
