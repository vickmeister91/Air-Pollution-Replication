The court order pertains to two appeals filed against an award passed by the Commissioner under Workmen's Compensation Act in W.C. Case No. 27 of 2000. The claimant, who is the wife of the deceased forest guard, had filed an application for compensation on the ground of her husband's death while on duty. The Commissioner had awarded Rs. 1,42,680/- as compensation, which was challenged by the appellants. The court held that the deceased forest guard can be treated as a workman as defined under the Act and his wife is entitled to the claim. The court also noted that the claimant had already been granted ex gratia for her husband's death while on duty, apart from the family pension, and therefore, she is not entitled to any further compensation.
