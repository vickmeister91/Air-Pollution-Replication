The court has granted bail to Vishesh Kumar, who was arrested in connection with FIR No. 219/2019 of Police Station Nathdwara, District Rajsamand, for the offence punishable under Sections 384, 387, 389, 419, 420 & 120B of IPC and Sections 9 & 51 of Wild Life Protection Act.
