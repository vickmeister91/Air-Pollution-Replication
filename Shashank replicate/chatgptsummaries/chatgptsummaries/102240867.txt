In November 2018, a criminal original petition was filed by G.M. Fawzunissa against the Inspector of Police and others, requesting the registration of a complaint and filing of an FIR. However, the court ruled that the petition was not maintainable and granted the petitioner liberty to seek remedy in accordance with guidelines given in a previous decision. The petition was disposed of on November 30, 2018.
