The Patna High Court has granted bail to Gobind Kumar Chaudhary, who was apprehending arrest in connection with a case registered under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Mines and Minerals Concession Rules-1972, and Prevention of Pollution Act-1986. The petitioner claimed that he was falsely implicated in the case by the personnel of Mining Department due to ulterior motive. The court ordered his release on bail on furnishing a bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Aurangabad.
