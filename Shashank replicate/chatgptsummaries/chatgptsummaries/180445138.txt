On March 5, 2019, Justice Rajesh Shankar passed an order pertaining to a petition filed by Arun Kumar Singh seeking to quash the criminal proceeding against him under Section 15 of the Environment Protection Act, 1986. The court noted that similar petitions filed by co-accused persons were allowed by the court in 2012. The court found that the present case was also covered by the previous orders and quashed the criminal proceeding against the petitioner.

The Environment Protection Act, 1986, is an important legislation that provides for the protection and improvement of the environment. Section 15 of the Act deals with the punishment for contravention of the provisions of the Act or the rules made thereunder. The section provides for imprisonment for a term which may extend to five years, or with fine which may extend to one lakh rupees, or with both. The section also provides for a higher punishment in case of a subsequent offence.

In the present case, Arun Kumar Singh was facing criminal proceedings under Section 15 of the Act. He had filed a petition seeking to quash the proceedings against him. The court noted that similar petitions filed by co-accused persons were allowed by the court in 2012. The court found that the present case was also covered by the previous orders and quashed the criminal proceeding against the petitioner.

The court's decision was based on the principle of res judicata, which means that a matter that has been adjudicated by a competent court cannot be re-agitated between the same parties. The court found that the present case was covered by the previous orders and that the petitioner was entitled to the same relief as the co-accused persons.

The court's decision is significant as it reaffirms the importance of the principle of res judicata in the Indian legal system. The principle ensures that there is finality in legal proceedings and that parties cannot keep re-agitating the same matter in different forums. This helps in reducing the burden on the courts and ensures that justice is delivered in a timely manner.
