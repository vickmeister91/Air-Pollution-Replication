The Patna High Court has granted bail to Nagendra Kumar, who was apprehensive of his arrest in connection with a case registered for offenses punishable under Sections 268, 270, 278, 308, and 326 of the Indian Penal Code. The case was registered for running a brick kiln without following the procedure under Section 21 of Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. The court granted bail to Kumar on furnishing a bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the Judicial Magistrate 1st Class, Patnacity, subject to the conditions laid down under Section 438(2) of the Cr.P.C.
