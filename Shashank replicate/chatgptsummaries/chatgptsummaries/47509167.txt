It is important to note that pre-arrest bail is granted to a person who apprehends arrest in a non-bailable offence. The purpose of pre-arrest bail is to protect the person from unnecessary harassment and detention by the police. The grant of pre-arrest bail is subject to certain conditions, such as cooperation with the investigation and not tampering with evidence.
