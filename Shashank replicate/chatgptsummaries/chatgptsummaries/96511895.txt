The court order pertains to an anticipatory bail application filed by Sujit Shrirang Gaikwad in connection with a case registered against him at Vaduj Police Station, District Satara, under section 379 r/w. 34 of IPC and under section 9 and 15 of the Environment Protection Act, 1986. The FIR alleges that Gaikwad was involved in the illegal transportation of sand. 

The court heard arguments from both sides and ultimately granted bail to Gaikwad on the condition that he attend the concerned Police Station and cooperate with the investigation. The court noted that the case against Gaikwad did not go beyond suspicion and that there was no evidence connecting him to the illegal excavation and transportation of sand.
