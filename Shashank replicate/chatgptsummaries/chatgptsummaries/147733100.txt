The court order in question pertains to a writ petition filed by Boothu Ratnamma against the State of Andhra Pradesh. The petitioner claimed that revenue authorities were taking steps to grant DKT pattas for forest land in Kadagunta Village, Balayapalli Mandal, SPSR Nellore District, at the behest of political affiliates. The petitioner sought a direction from the court to prevent the allotment of forest land for any purpose. The court allowed the writ petition and directed the revenue authorities not to allot the forest land for any purpose whatsoever.
