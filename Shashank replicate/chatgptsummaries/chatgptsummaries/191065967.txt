In a recent court order, a writ petition filed by three village panchayats seeking to quash an order by the District Collector permitting the installation of pump wells in three villages to supply water to Mandapam Town Panchayat was dismissed. The petitioners argued that the scheme would deprive villagers of their water rights and violate environmental protection laws. However, the court considered reports from geological experts and the State Ground Water Division, which found that the scheme was feasible and would not lead to salinity or sea water intrusion.

The court's decision was based on a thorough enquiry conducted by the District Collector, who provided detailed reasoning for the decision. The court found that the Collector had taken into account all relevant factors and had made an informed decision based on the available evidence.
