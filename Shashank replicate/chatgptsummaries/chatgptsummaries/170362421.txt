After hearing the arguments of both sides, the court allowed the bail application and directed the applicants to furnish a personal bond of Rs.1,00,000/- each with two local sureties each in the like amount. The applicants were also required to comply with certain conditions, including cooperating in the investigation/trial and not committing any similar offence.
