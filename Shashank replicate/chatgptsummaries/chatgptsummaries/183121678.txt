The court has granted bail to Mukesh Mahto, who has been accused of various offences including illegal quarrying of stones and possession of explosive substances. The petitioner has been in custody since May 2018 and the charge sheet has been submitted. The court has directed the petitioner to furnish a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 and two sureties of the same amount each to the satisfaction of the learned C.J.M., Hazaribagh, subject to the condition that he will deposit Rs. 15,000 with the District Mining Officer, Hazaribagh and furnish the receipt thereof in the Court below at the time of furnishing bail bond.
