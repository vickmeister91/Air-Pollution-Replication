The Board had issued a direction order for stoppage of electricity and closure of the industry. However, the petitioner had approached the Madras High Court seeking a writ of mandamus to direct the respondents to take necessary action against the illegal unit and close it permanently.

The court also noted that the Board had already issued a direction order for stoppage of electricity and closure of the industry. Therefore, there was no need to issue any mandamus.
