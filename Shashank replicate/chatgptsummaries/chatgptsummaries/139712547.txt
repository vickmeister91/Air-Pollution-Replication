The National Green Tribunal is a specialized court that deals with environmental disputes and issues. It was established in 2010 under the National Green Tribunal Act, and has jurisdiction over all civil cases involving environmental issues. The tribunal has the power to hear cases related to air and water pollution, waste management, forest conservation, and biodiversity conservation, among others.
