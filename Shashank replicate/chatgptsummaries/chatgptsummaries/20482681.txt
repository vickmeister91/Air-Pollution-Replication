The Patna High Court has granted anticipatory bail to Virit Bind in connection with a case filed against him under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Mines Act, and Prevention of Pollution Act. The petitioner claimed to be the owner of a seized Eicher Tractor and that the sand was loaded with a valid challan. The court allowed the bail on the condition that the petitioner surrenders within six weeks and furnishes a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 with two sureties. The bailors should be local and have sufficient immovable property, and the petitioner must cooperate in the trial and not tamper with evidence or witnesses.
