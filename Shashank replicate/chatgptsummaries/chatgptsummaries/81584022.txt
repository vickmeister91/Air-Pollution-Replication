The court order in question pertains to a criminal misc. petition filed by Suwa Lal against the order passed by the Special Judge, NDPS Act Cases, Bhilwara in Sessions Case No.19/2016. The petitioner had filed an application under Section 91 Cr.P.C. seeking call details, which was dismissed by the trial court. The petitioner's counsel argued that the case was covered by a previous judgment of the court.

The court, after hearing the arguments of both parties, directed the trial court to safeguard the call details and keep them in a sealed cover in the record of the court, if available. The court also allowed the trial court to look into the available data as and when necessary. The court disposed of the petition and the stay application.
