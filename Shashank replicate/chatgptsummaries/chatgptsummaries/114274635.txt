Anticipatory bail is a provision in the Indian legal system that allows a person to seek bail in anticipation of an arrest. This provision is available to a person who apprehends arrest for a non-bailable offence. The purpose of anticipatory bail is to protect the person from unnecessary harassment and detention by the police.

The bail is subject to the conditions laid down under Section 438(2) of the Cr.P.C.
