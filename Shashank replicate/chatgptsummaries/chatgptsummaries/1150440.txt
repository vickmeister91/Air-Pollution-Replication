The Madras High Court recently dismissed a writ petition filed by R. Vasantha Rajendran, who sought a writ of mandamus to stop or control noise pollution caused by the use of amplifiers and loudspeakers outside the Natesar Alayam, Illathar Samudayam, Manavalakurichy, Kanyakumari District. The court noted that the petitioner had already approached the civil court and obtained a decree in his favour, which had attained finality. The court also observed that the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board had issued instructions to control noise pollution, and it was for the petitioner to seek remedy under the Pollution Control Act for violation of the order. The writ petition was dismissed with costs.
