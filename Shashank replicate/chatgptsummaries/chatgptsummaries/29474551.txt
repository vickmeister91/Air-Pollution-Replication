Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, deals with the dishonour of cheques for insufficiency of funds in the account of the drawer. The provision makes it a criminal offence punishable with imprisonment or a fine or both. In such cases, the complainant is required to prove that the cheque was issued for a debt or liability, and that it was dishonoured due to insufficient funds in the account of the drawer.
