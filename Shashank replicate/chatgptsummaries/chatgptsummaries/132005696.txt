The court has granted bail to Bheekharam @ Bheekhla, who was arrested for an offence under Section 9/51 Wild Animal Life Protection Act & 27 Arms Act in FIR No. 125/2016 of Police Station, Kolayat, District Bikaner. The bail is granted under Section 439 Cr.P.C. and the accused must execute a personal bond of Rs. 50,000 with two sureties of Rs. 25,000 each to appear before the trial court on every date of hearing until the completion of the trial.

Bheekharam @ Bheekhla was arrested for an offence under Section 9/51 Wild Animal Life Protection Act & 27 Arms Act in FIR No. 125/2016 of Police Station, Kolayat, District Bikaner. The accused was charged with illegal possession of arms and ammunition and hunting of wild animals. The accused was produced before the court and remanded to judicial custody. The accused filed a bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. before the court.

The court considered the bail application and observed that the accused was in judicial custody for more than six months. The court also observed that the investigation in the case was complete and the charge sheet had been filed. The court further observed that the accused was not a habitual offender and had no criminal antecedents. The court also took into consideration the fact that the accused was a resident of the area and had a fixed place of abode. The court also noted that the accused had cooperated with the investigation and had not absconded or tampered with the evidence.

The court, therefore, granted bail to the accused under Section 439 Cr.P.C. The accused was directed to execute a personal bond of Rs. 50,000 with two sureties of Rs. 25,000 each to appear before the trial court on every date of hearing until the completion of the trial. The court also directed the accused to surrender his passport, if any, and not to leave the country without the permission of the court. The court also directed the accused not to tamper with the evidence or influence the witnesses in any manner.
