In a recent court order, a case involving the seizure of a vehicle in connection with a wildlife protection act violation was addressed. The petitioner, who is a student and the power of attorney holder of his brother, requested the release of the vehicle on furnishing superdnama. The court allowed the petition and reduced the bank guarantee to Rs. 1,50,000/- and enhanced the superdnama to Rs. 5,00,000/-. The other conditions remained the same.
