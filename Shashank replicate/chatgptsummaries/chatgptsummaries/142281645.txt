The case in question involves a Criminal Original Petition filed by the petitioner to set aside an order made in Crl.M.P.No.548 of 2010, in C.C.No.2444 of 2009, on the file of VIII Metropolitan Magistrate, George Town, Chennai. The petitioner had filed a petition under Section 91 Cr.P.C. for production of documents, which was partly allowed by the learned Magistrate who directed the petitioner to produce certain documents. Dissatisfied with this decision, the petitioner filed the present Criminal Original Petition.

The Trial Court, however, rightly allowed the Petition and dismissed the Criminal Original Petition. The VIII Metropolitan Magistrate, George Town, Chennai, was directed to dispose of the said case within a period of four months from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
