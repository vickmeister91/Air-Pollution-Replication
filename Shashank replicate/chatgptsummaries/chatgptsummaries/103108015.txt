Anticipatory bail is a provision in the Indian legal system that allows a person to seek bail in anticipation of being arrested. The court must consider several factors when deciding whether or not to grant anticipatory bail, including the nature and gravity of the offense, the likelihood of the accused fleeing from justice, and the possibility of the accused tampering with evidence.
