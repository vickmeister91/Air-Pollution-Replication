The Rajasthan High Court recently dismissed four revision petitions that challenged charges under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. The petitioners argued that there was no sanction for the charges and that the reports of the analyst could not be considered. However, the court found that there was a general sanction and that the analyst was appointed before the notification was published. The court also found that the inspection reports and evidence were sufficient to support the charges. Therefore, the court dismissed the revision petitions.
