The court order in question pertains to a petition filed by Sanjay Mehta seeking to quash the First Information Report (FIR) filed against him in a case related to the illegal establishment of crusher machines without proper licenses. The court dismissed the petition, stating that it is for the investigating officer to determine whether the petitioner was involved in the illegal offense or not.

The case in question revolves around the establishment of crusher machines without proper licenses. Sanjay Mehta, the petitioner, was accused of being involved in the illegal establishment of these machines. He filed a petition seeking to quash the FIR filed against him, stating that he was not involved in the offense.

The court, however, dismissed the petition, stating that it is for the investigating officer to determine whether the petitioner was involved in the offense or not. The court also noted that the petitioner had not provided any evidence to support his claim that he was not involved in the offense.
