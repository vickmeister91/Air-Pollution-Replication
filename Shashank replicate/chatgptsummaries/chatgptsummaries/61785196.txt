The court order in question pertains to a criminal original petition filed under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. The petition was filed to set aside an order made in Crl.MP.No.4817 of 2017 in STC.No.12 of 2015 on the file of the Judicial Magistrate Court No.II, Pollachi. The complainant had initiated a prosecution for the offence under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, against the accused. During cross-examination, the complainant conceded that there was a document in respect of the transaction between the accused and the complainant's wife. 

The accused filed CMP.No.4817 of 2017 in STC.No.12 of 2015 under Section 91 Cr.P.C., to produce the said document by the complainant. The trial court allowed the CMP, and the complainant filed the present petition to set aside the order. The court directed the complainant to produce the original and photo copy of the said document, and after complying with the same, the original may be returned to the complainant, and the copy may be marked as an Exhibit.
