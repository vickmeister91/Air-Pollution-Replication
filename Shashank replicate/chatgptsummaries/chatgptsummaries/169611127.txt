The court order pertains to a Criminal Original Petition filed by the petitioner under Section 482 of Cr.P.C. The petitioner sought direction to the Inspector of Police, Kannivadi Police Station, Dindigul District to investigate the case in Crime No.331 of 2017 pending on the file of the respondent. The petitioner requested the police to summon the accused persons concerned with the above said case from the Court of law by invoking Section 91 of Cr.P.C. and further direct him to file a final report within the stipulated period as prescribed by the Court.

The court directed the respondent police to complete the investigation in Crime No.331 of 2017 within a period of three months from the date of receipt of a copy of this order. The police were instructed to either file a final report or closure report, as the case may be, under due intimation of the defacto complainant. The Criminal Original Petition was disposed of.

The court order is significant as it highlights the importance of timely investigation and filing of reports in criminal cases. The court has directed the police to complete the investigation within a stipulated period of three months and file a final report or closure report accordingly. This ensures that justice is delivered in a timely manner and the accused are not kept in custody for an extended period without a proper trial.

The court has also emphasized the need for the police to invoke Section 91 of Cr.P.C. to summon the accused persons concerned with the case from the Court of law. This provision empowers the police to obtain necessary documents and information from any person or authority, including the Court, to aid in the investigation of a case. By invoking this provision, the police can ensure that all relevant information is obtained and the investigation is conducted in a thorough and comprehensive manner.

Overall, the court order serves as a reminder to the police and other law enforcement agencies to conduct investigations in a timely and efficient manner. It also highlights the importance of invoking relevant provisions of the law to aid in the investigation process. By doing so, justice can be delivered in a fair and timely manner, ensuring that the rights of both the accused and the victim are protected.
