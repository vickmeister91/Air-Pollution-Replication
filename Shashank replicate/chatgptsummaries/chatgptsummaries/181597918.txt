In case number 624/12, the State had filed a case against Rajesh Bhatia and S.K. Gosain, both directors of Rosmarine Shipping Pvt. Ltd, for not complying with a notice under Section 91 Cr.P.C. to produce certain documents. The accused pleaded not guilty and claimed trial.
