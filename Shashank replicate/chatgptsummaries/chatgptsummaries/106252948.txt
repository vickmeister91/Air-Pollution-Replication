Section 127(1) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, deals with the alteration of maintenance orders. In this case, the petitioner claimed that there had been a change in circumstances that warranted a revision of the order for interim maintenance.

Therefore, the court dismissed the criminal revision filed by the petitioner.
