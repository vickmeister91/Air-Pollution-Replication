The court recently dismissed a writ petition filed by Ashwani Kumar against the Punjab State Electricity Board. The petitioner had been directed to supply details about the operation of saw mills in other areas, but failed to comply with the directions. The impugned notices related to the setting up of saw mills in a land which was notified as forest under the Forest Conservation Act, 1980. The direction for closure of the saw mills was challenged as being discriminatory among other grounds, but the court found no error in the impugned notices given.
