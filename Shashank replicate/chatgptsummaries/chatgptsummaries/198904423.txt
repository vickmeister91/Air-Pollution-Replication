However, the Commissioner is ready to attend the court proceedings on March 1, 2019, and is expected to comply with the directions contained in the order dated January 8, 2014, passed by the Division Bench of the Court.

The Civil Application was allowed and disposed of, and the Public Interest Litigation was listed for March 1, 2019. This order was passed by the Aurangabad Bench of the Bombay High Court in response to a petition filed by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation seeking exemption from attending court proceedings in a public interest litigation.

The public interest litigation pertains to the issue of illegal constructions in the city of Aurangabad. The petitioner had alleged that the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation had failed to take action against illegal constructions despite repeated complaints. The petitioner had also alleged that the Corporation had failed to comply with the directions contained in the order dated January 8, 2014, passed by the Division Bench of the Court.

The order dated January 8, 2014, had directed the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation to take action against illegal constructions and to submit a report to the Court on the progress made in this regard. The Court had also directed the Corporation to take steps to prevent illegal constructions in the future.

In response to the petition filed by the Aurangabad Municipal Corporation, the Court had directed the Commissioner of the Corporation to attend the court proceedings and to explain the steps taken by the Corporation to comply with the directions contained in the order dated January 8, 2014. However, due to a conflicting hearing in an appeal under the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972, the Commissioner was granted exemption from attending the court proceedings on February 15, 2019.

The Court has listed the public interest litigation for March 1, 2019, and the Commissioner is expected to attend the court proceedings on this date. The Court has also directed the Commissioner to comply with the directions contained in the order dated January 8, 2014, and to take steps to prevent illegal constructions in the future.
