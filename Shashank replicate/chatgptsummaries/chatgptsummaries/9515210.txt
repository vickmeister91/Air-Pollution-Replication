The court order in question pertains to a case of sand excavation from a river bed in violation of various laws. The petitioner, Devendra Kumar Upadhyaye, had filed an application for anticipatory bail under section 438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. The court heard the arguments of both sides and found that there was no evidence to implicate the petitioner in the case. Therefore, the court allowed the application for anticipatory bail and directed that in case of arrest, the petitioner shall be released on bail on furnishing a personal bond and one solvent surety.
