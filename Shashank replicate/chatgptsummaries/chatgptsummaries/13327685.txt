The petitioner has sought the quashment of Annexure 2 forest offence seizure report prepared under Section 52 of the Kerala Forest Act, 1961, and all further proceedings thereunder.

The prosecution's case is that a vehicle was taken to the reserve forest and dumped plastic and other scrap materials, causing ecological disturbances. The petitioner has denied any involvement in the alleged offense and has sought to have the charges against him dropped.

However, the court has dismissed the petitioner's plea and advised him to argue for a discharge in the lower court. This means that the petitioner will have to present his case before a lower court and argue for his discharge from the charges against him.

The court's decision to dismiss the petitioner's plea is based on the fact that the petitioner has not been able to provide sufficient evidence to support his claim of innocence. The court has noted that the protection of the environment is of utmost importance and that any offense that causes harm to the environment must be dealt with strictly.
