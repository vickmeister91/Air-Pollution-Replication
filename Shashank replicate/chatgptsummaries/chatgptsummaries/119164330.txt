The court order in question pertains to two applications filed by the accused in a case where he was summoned for an offense punishable under section 138 of the NI Act. The accused had filed two applications, one seeking admission/denial of certain documents and another seeking production of certain documents from the complainant. However, the court dismissed both applications, stating that the accused cannot rely on documents at the stage of notice or charge.

The court further stated that section 91 of the Cr.P.C. cannot be restored at the stage of notice since the defense of the accused is not relevant at this stage. The accused can resort to these sections only at the stage of his defense. The case was put up for framing of notice on 27.08.09.
