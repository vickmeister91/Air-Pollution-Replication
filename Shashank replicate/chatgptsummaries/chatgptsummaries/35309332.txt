The court order pertains to an application for an extension of time for compliance with an order dated 02.04.2018 passed in M.Cr.C. No.9850/2018. The application was filed on behalf of the applicant, Mahendra Sonwane, who is apprehending his arrest in connection with Crime No/POR No.(Forest) 11950/23 registered at Police Station Forest Range Lanji, District Balaghat for the offence punishable under Sections 2(16)(7), 9, 39 (1)(b), 39 (3), 44(1) 39-A (b) 39-B (III), 50 51 and 52 of Wild Life Protection Act, 1972.

The court allowed the application and directed the applicant to appear before the trial court within 15 days from the date of receipt of the certified copy of the order. In the event of arrest, the applicant, Mahendra Sonwane, shall be released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond in the sum of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees Forty Thousand), with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court.

The case in question pertains to a violation of the Wild Life Protection Act, 1972. The Act was enacted to provide for the protection of wild animals, birds, and plants, and for matters connected therewith or ancillary or incidental thereto. The Act aims to ensure the protection of wildlife and their habitats, as well as to regulate hunting, poaching, and trade in wildlife and their products.

The specific sections of the Act under which the applicant has been charged are as follows:

- Section 2(16)(7): Defines "wildlife" to include any animal, bees, butterflies, crustacean, fish and moths, and aquatic or land vegetation which forms part of any habitat.
- Section 9: Prohibits hunting of wild animals, except as provided under the Act.
- Section 39 (1)(b): Prohibits the possession, sale, or transport of any wild animal, animal article, trophy, or uncured trophy.
- Section 39 (3): Prohibits the possession, sale, or transport of any captive animal, animal article, trophy, or uncured trophy.
- Section 44(1): Provides for the punishment for hunting in a sanctuary or national park.
- Section 39-A (b): Provides for the punishment for the illegal trade in wildlife and their products.
- Section 39-B (III): Provides for the punishment for the illegal possession of wildlife and their products.
- Section 50: Provides for the punishment for the contravention of any provision of the Act or any rule, order, or permit issued thereunder.
- Section 51: Provides for the punishment for abetment of any offence under the Act.
- Section 52: Provides for the punishment for attempts to commit any offence under the Act.
