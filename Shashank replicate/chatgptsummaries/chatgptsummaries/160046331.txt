The court order in question pertains to a petition filed by Atul Singh under Section 482 of Cr.P.C. against an order passed by the Court of Special Judge SC/ST (Atrocities) Act, Sidhi. The applicant had filed an application under Section 457 of Cr.P.C. for taking possession of a seized vehicle UP-95-T-0357 for temporary superdaginama. The court found that no confiscation proceedings had been initiated by the respective departments and directed the Court of JMFC Churhat District Sidhi to enquire and release the vehicle on "Superdaginama" to the applicant.
