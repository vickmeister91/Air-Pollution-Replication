The Patna High Court has granted anticipatory bail to four petitioners who were apprehending their arrest in a case registered under various sections of the Indian Penal Code, Bihar Minor Mineral Concession Rules, Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, Indian Forest Act, Water (Prevention and Control) Pollution Act, Air (Prevention and Control) Pollution Act, and Environment (Protection) Act. The FIR was lodged against 19 persons who were running a crusher without a valid license. The court considered the nature of seizure and granted bail to the petitioners on the condition of furnishing a bail bond of Rs. 10,000 each with two sureties.
