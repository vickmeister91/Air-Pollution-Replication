In a recent court order, the Karnataka High Court has directed the respondents to reconsider the application of Francies Georges for a stone quarrying license. The court order pertains to a writ petition filed by Georges seeking to quash an endorsement rejecting his application for the license. The court did not make any adjudication on the issue of the petitioner's entitlement to the license. However, the court's order is significant as it highlights the importance of following due process and considering all relevant factors while making decisions on license applications.
