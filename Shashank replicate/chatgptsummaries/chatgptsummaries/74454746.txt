The court has granted bail to Munna, who was arrested on 14/3/2018 for hunting wild boar without authority, under the Wild Life Protection Act. Munna has no criminal antecedents and his further custody is not required. He has been ordered to furnish a personal bond of Rs.25,000/- with a solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court and comply with the conditions enumerated in sub-section (3) of Section 437 of Cr.P.C.
