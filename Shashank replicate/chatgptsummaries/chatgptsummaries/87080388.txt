The court order in question pertains to a writ petition filed by Chandra Prakash Sharma, who runs a hotel in Uttarakhand. The hotel had been sealed by the Uttarakhand Environment Pollution Control Board, and the petitioner had earlier filed an appeal before the National Green Tribunal, seeking permission to establish a Sewage Treatment Plant (STP). The Tribunal granted the petitioner the liberty to establish the STP, but the sealing order was not withdrawn. 

In response to this, the petitioner filed a writ petition before the Uttarakhand High Court, seeking relief against the sealing order. The court disposed of the writ petition with a direction to the Board to consider the matter in light of the Tribunal's directions and pass appropriate orders within three weeks.
